The People's Refinement Logic

The RedPRL Development Team has developed a number of proof assistants for Cartesian cubical type theory at Carnegie Mellon University under the supervision of Robert Harper.

Open-source contributors are the shock troops of the revolution! Join us now.

cooltt

We are currently developing cooltt, a proof assistant for Cartesian cubical type theory. It is available on GitHub. The internals are lightly documented here.

redtt

redtt is a proof assistant for Cartesian cubical type theory. It is available on GitHub.

RedPRL

RedPRL is a proof assistant for computational Cartesian cubical type theory inspired by Nuprl. It is written in Standard ML, and is available for download on GitHub. Documentation is available here.

Contributors

RedPRL is developed by Carlo Angiuli, Evan Cavallo, Kuen-Bang Hou (Favonia) and Jon Sterling under the supervision of Robert Harper. Full Contributors.
